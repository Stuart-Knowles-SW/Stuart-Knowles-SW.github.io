Joey Gold

Joey really is a wonderful guy. Although his origins are unknown, he has watched over the world with a careful eye for centuries.

When he is not acting as eternal guardian of the human race, Joey enjoys table football and playing in brass bands. He has also been known to enjoy hummus on occasion, though it has unpredictable effects on his superhuman physiology.
